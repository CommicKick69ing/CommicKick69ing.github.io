Notice of termination of network communication battle service

Thank you for playing "Sakura Wars Columns 2" on a regular basis.

The "Sakura Wars Columns 2" network match, which began on January 6, 2000,
will end on August 31, 2001.

To the Sakura Wars Columns 2 fans who played during the service's online period,
Thank you very much for playing. We would like to also thank everyone from the development staff.

The service usage fee will be free from July 3rd to August 31st

* The server usage fee is free, but you need to obtain the free usage rights.
* The customer will be responsible for the communication fee for network battles (KDDI line usage fee 10 yen / minute).
Please be careful.

In addition, after the network communication battle service ends,
the software will still be able to play offline, Thank you for your continued patronage.


Network service has ended.
July 2, 2001
